About me

I took great interest in the software field while I was following my first major in Graphic Design and Multimedia, where I learned about web design and its principles.

I finished my studies and worked as a Graphic Designer but after one year I decided to follow another major, something that would strengthen my knowledge about back end and programming of software, a degree in Computation and Systems Engineering.

Later on, the software development department of the university offered me a position to work with them. I gained extensive hands-on experience developing web applications by using different programming languages including HTML/CSS, Javascript, PHP, ExtJs.

I consider myself fortunate to see how information shared is bigger than ever and complex processes are now simpler thanks to the internet. Everyone is connected now. What I want is to create better web systems, with a solid understanding of the process behind it.
